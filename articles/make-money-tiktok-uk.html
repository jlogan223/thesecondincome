How to Make Money on TikTok in the UK in 2026

TikTok is now the second most searched money-making topic in the UK. But the reality is more nuanced than the viral success stories suggest. Here's an honest breakdown of how UK creators actually make money, what it realistically takes, and whether it's worth your time.

The Second Income UK•9 min read•£50–£2,000+/mo

The Four Ways to Earn Money on TikTok

1. TikTok Creator Rewards Program
TikTok pays creators based on video performance. To qualify you need at least 10,000 followers and 100,000 views in the last 30 days. Pay rates vary but typically sit at £0.02–£0.04 per 1,000 views, meaning a video with 100,000 views earns around £2–£4. This alone won't make you rich.

2. TikTok LIVE Gifts
During live streams, viewers can send virtual gifts that convert to real money. Some UK creators earn £50–£500+ per live session once they have an engaged audience. Requires 1,000+ followers to go live.

3. Brand Deals and Sponsored Content
This is where real TikTok income comes from. UK creators with 10,000–50,000 followers can charge £100–£500 per sponsored post. Those with 100,000+ followers typically earn £500–£5,000 per brand deal. Platforms like Creator.co and Influencer.com connect smaller creators with brands.

4. TikTok Shop Affiliate
Promote products from TikTok Shop in your videos and earn 5–20% commission on each sale. This has become the fastest-growing income stream for UK TikTok creators in 2026, you don't need a huge audience, just content that converts.

What You Actually Need to Start Earning

Honest timeline: most creators don't earn meaningful income until 6–12 months in. The exception is TikTok Shop affiliate, a well-placed product review with 5,000 views can generate £50–£200 in commission within days.

The niches that monetise best in the UK: personal finance (ironically), cleaning and home organisation, fashion and beauty, cooking, and small business/entrepreneurship content.

Is TikTok Worth Starting as a Side Hustle?

If you're already spending time on TikTok, starting to create content costs nothing and has real upside. If you're starting from scratch purely for money, it's a long game, expect 6–18 months before meaningful income. The creators who do best treat it as a platform to build an audience that they then monetise through multiple channels (their own products, services, affiliate links).

Frequently Asked Questions

How many followers do you need to make money on TikTok?

You can make money with very few followers through TikTok Shop affiliate, a well-placed product review can earn commissions with any audience size. The Creator Rewards Program requires 10,000 followers minimum.

How much does TikTok pay per view in the UK?

The Creator Rewards Program pays approximately £0.02–£0.04 per 1,000 views. A video with 1 million views might earn £20–£40, brand deals and TikTok Shop are far more lucrative.

What type of TikTok content makes the most money in the UK?

TikTok Shop affiliate content (product reviews and demonstrations) earns the most relative to audience size. Finance, beauty, food, and home/lifestyle niches attract the most brand deal budgets.
