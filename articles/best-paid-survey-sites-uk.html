Best Paid Survey Sites in the UK for 2026 (Ranked by Hourly Rate)

We tested every major UK survey platform over three months and ranked them by what you actually earn per hour — not the inflated figures they advertise.

Prolific — our top pick

Prolific is the standout choice for paid surveys in the UK. Unlike most survey platforms that pay pennies, Prolific has a minimum pay rate policy of roughly £6.50/hour and many studies pay significantly more. Studies are clearly labelled with estimated time and pay before you start — no surprises.

The catch is availability — studies fill up quickly and you can't always get on when you want. The trick is to keep notifications turned on and respond quickly when new studies appear.

How to join Prolific: Visit prolific.com and add yourself to the waitlist — they periodically open registration to new participants. It is worth waiting as the pay rates are the best available on any UK survey platform.

Other solid options

YouGov — well-known, consistent supply of surveys, lower pay rate (around £2–£3/hour effective). Good for passive background earnings. Redeemable for cash or gift cards.

Swagbucks — more than just surveys. Earn points (SBs) for watching videos, searching the web, and completing offers. The hourly rate varies wildly but the diversity of tasks means you can always find something to do.

Pinecone Research — invitation only but pays a flat £3 per survey with no screening rejections. Worth joining if you get an invite.

What can you realistically earn?

Fifteen to thirty minutes on Prolific each day, responding quickly to new studies, realistically earns £50–£100/month. Adding YouGov and Swagbucks alongside adds another £20–£50. This is not a primary income — it is effortless background money that compounds over time.

The sweet spot is treating survey sites as something you do while watching TV or waiting for something else. Forcing yourself to sit down specifically for surveys is demoralising given the rates.

Tips to maximise your earnings

Turn on Prolific notifications and respond within the first few minutes of a study going live — popular studies fill in under an hour. Keep your demographic profile complete and honest — studies are targeted by profile, and a complete profile means more invitations. On Swagbucks, the daily goal bonus (a small multiplier for hitting a points target) adds up meaningfully over a month. Withdraw regularly rather than letting balances sit — platforms occasionally change terms.
