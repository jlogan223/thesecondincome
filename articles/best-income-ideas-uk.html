The 15 Best Ways to Earn a Second Income in the UK for 2026

Ranked by realistic earning potential, how quickly you can start, and how much ongoing effort each one actually takes, written for people with a full-time job.

Updated April 2026 · 12 min read · Up to £2,000/mo

There has never been more opportunity to earn money alongside a day job in the UK. But not all income ideas are equal, some take months to pay off, others can generate cash within days. This guide ranks the most reliable options by what you can realistically expect to earn.

1

Cashback Apps

£100–£400/yr

The easiest money available, you earn cashback on purchases you'd make anyway. TopCashback and Quidco are the two best UK platforms. Takes 10 minutes to set up and earns passively from day one.

⏱ 10 mins to start 💰 Zero risk 🎯 Completely passive

2

Matched Betting

£500–£1,500/mo

The highest consistent earner on this list. Uses bookmaker promotions mathematically to lock in profit regardless of sporting outcomes. Legal, but listed under High Risk due to the gambling platforms involved, discipline and the right software are essential.

⏱ 1–2 hrs to start ⚠ High Risk category 🎯 5–10 hrs/week
Full guide: Is Matched Betting Worth It? →
3

Paid Surveys

£50–£200/mo

Genuinely easy extra cash with zero risk. Prolific pays the best rates in the UK (typically £5–£12/hour). Not life-changing money but completely effortless alongside other income streams.

⏱ 5 mins to start 💰 Zero risk 🎯 15–30 mins/day
Full guide: Best Paid Survey Sites UK → (coming soon)
4

Selling on eBay

£100–£2,000/mo

Start by clearing your home, most people have £200–£500 worth of unused items. From there, many move into reselling from car boots, charity shops, and clearance stores. Highly scalable with time.

⏱ 30 mins to start 💰 Low risk 🎯 2–10 hrs/week
Full guide: How to Make Money on eBay UK → (coming soon)
5

Freelancing

£200–£1,000/mo

If you have any professional skill, writing, design, bookkeeping, virtual assistance, social media, you can sell it on platforms like PeoplePerHour, Fiverr, or Upwork. 2–3 regular clients can bring in £500–£1,000/month alongside full-time work.

⏱ 1 hr to start 💰 Low risk 🎯 Flexible hours
