How accurate are these tools? Every figure used here comes directly from gov.uk, HMRC, gov.scot, or HM Treasury, and is cited on each tool's page. The underlying calculation logic is unit-tested against HMRC's own worked examples. That said, these are guides not personal tax advice. For your specific circumstances, consult HMRC or a qualified UK accountant.

When are these updated? Tax rates change at each Budget. We update these tools at the start of each new tax year (6 April) once HMRC publishes the new figures. The current versions reflect Autumn Budget 2025 (the most recent fiscal event) and all rates effective from 6 April 2026.
