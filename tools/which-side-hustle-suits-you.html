How the quiz works

Every side hustle on TSI-UK is tagged with attributes: time required, startup budget, format (online/physical/active/passive), payoff speed, risk profile, skill needs, and whether you need to sell yourself. The quiz scores each side hustle against your answers and ranks the top three. Tie-breaks go to the option with the highest realistic monthly ceiling.

This is a guide. Your actual results depend on local demand, how much you commit, and timing. Pick a top match, read the full guide, then come back and re-take if your situation changes.
